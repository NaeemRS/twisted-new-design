Our heritage

A Glasgow House of Packaging & Hygiene

Twisted Hygiene & Packaging was founded on an enduring principle: that the vessels which carry your food and the standards that protect your kitchen are never mere commodities — they are extensions of your craft.

From 149 Woodhead Road, our team curates an extensive collection of premium stock lines and bespoke commissions, serving fine cafés, distinguished restaurants and ambitious caterers across the United Kingdom.
