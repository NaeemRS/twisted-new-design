Questions answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Orders placed before 3pm Monday to Friday dispatch the same day for next-working-day delivery on mainland UK. Delivery is free on orders over £100 excl. VAT. Standard carriage rates apply below that threshold and are shown at checkout before you pay.

Send us your logo or brand guidelines and tell us which product you need — cups, bags, boxes, or greaseproof. We prepare a digital proof for approval, confirm MOQ and lead time, then schedule production. Most custom jobs complete within 3–4 weeks depending on print method and volume.

All trade prices on our website are shown excluding VAT. VAT at the prevailing UK rate is added at checkout. If you are VAT registered, enter your VAT number in your account to receive VAT invoices suitable for your records.

Stock lines can be ordered in case quantities as listed on each product page — often from a single case. Custom printed packaging typically starts from 1,000 units on cups and 500–1,000 on bags, depending on size and print colours. Contact us for exact MOQs on your project.

If goods arrive damaged or incorrect, contact us within 48 hours with photos and your order number — we will arrange replacement or credit promptly. Custom-printed items cannot be returned unless faulty. Unused standard stock in original packaging may be returned within 14 days subject to a restocking fee.
