Our story

From Glasgow to kitchens across the UK

Twisted Hygiene & Packaging started with a simple promise: keep hospitality businesses stocked with quality packaging and cleaning supplies at honest prices — with people on the end of the phone when you need them.

Today we serve cafés, hotels, schools and caterers from our base at 149 Woodhead Road, shipping nationwide with the same hands-on approach we began with.
